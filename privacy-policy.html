Fanta F1 - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fanta F1 collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the app.

1. Data We Collect

Account and League Data

  • Display name and login credentials.
  • League information (league name, invite code, role, team name).
  • Team selections (drivers, captain, reserve), budget, and standings.

Technical Data

  • Basic app diagnostics and error information.
  • Device-level ad consent status and ad interaction events (via AdMob).
  • Purchase/subscription status (via RevenueCat and store providers).

2. How We Use Data

  • To create and manage your account and league experience.
  • To calculate scores, rankings, and race-related game features.
  • To process premium purchases and restore entitlements.
  • To show ads for free users and manage consent requirements.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve app reliability.

3. Third-Party Services

Fanta F1 uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase for backend and data storage.
  • Google AdMob for advertising.
  • RevenueCat for subscription/purchase management.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play for in-app purchase billing.
  • OpenF1 for race/sports data integration.

These providers may process data under their own privacy policies.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

Where required (for example in the EEA/UK), consent is requested before serving personalized ads. You can update consent choices in your device or app settings when available.

5. Data Retention

We keep account and gameplay data while your account remains active, and for a limited period as needed for legal, security, and operational reasons.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to reduce risk.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also request account deletion.

8. Children

Fanta F1 is not intended for children under the minimum digital consent age applicable in their jurisdiction.

9. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: dalpontgiuliano@hotmail.com
